Project Goal:

We aim to determine relationships between different genres of movies and their soundtracks by designing interactive visualizations that faciliate the exploration of our SpotifyMDB dataset.

Background Information:

Description of Each Visualization

  • Genre Similarity Matrix: Shows calculated distance between the average soundtrack of two genres
  • Radar Chart: Uses a polygon shape to contrast the average attributes of two genres
  • Scatterplot: The X and Y axis each show a musical attribute from the dataset. Every movie soundtrack in our dataset has its own point on the scatterplot. Points are color-encoded by genre. Hovering over a point show the movie title and the values of the two selected attributes.
