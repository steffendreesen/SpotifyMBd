Project Goal

We aim to identify relationships between different genres of movies and their soundtracks by designing interactive visualizations that faciliate the exploration of our SpotifyMDb dataset.

Recommended Use

1. Start by selecting a cell in the similarity matrix that seems interesting.

2. Using the radar, identify attributes where the two genres differ.

3. Update the scatter plot axes to the attributes identified from the radar.

4. Add or remove genres, adjust the minimum tracks required, and change the axis attributes to further explore the dataset!
